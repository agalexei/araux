My

Process

01 — Discover

I lead discovery to deeply understand customer needs, business goals, and technical constraints. Through stakeholder alignment, user research, journey mapping, and data analysis, I define problem spaces, surface opportunities, and establish clear direction for design.

02 — Design

I translate insights into considered product experiences, moving from concepts to high-fidelity prototypes. I balance user needs, behavioural insight, and business objectives to design clear, scalable solutions that are grounded in evidence and ready for delivery.

03 — Refine

I validate ideas through testing and iteration, working closely with engineers to bring designs to life. I refine flows, interactions, and visual hierarchy to ensure accessibility, usability, and quality to deliver experiences that perform in real-world conditions.
