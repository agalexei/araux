NRMA Home Quote & Buy Redesign

At Insurance Australia Group (NRMA), I lead end-to-end product design for the Home Insurance Quote & Buy experience across Home & Contents, Home Only, and Contents Only journeys. My role spans discovery through delivery, combining customer research, UX, UI, and interaction design to create clearer, faster, and higher-converting experiences. Most recently, I contributed to a redesign that reduced time-to-quote by approximately 50%, helping improve both conversion and customer confidence by simplifying language, reducing friction, and improving key decision points.

I work closely with Product Managers, Business Analysts, Engineers, Content, Behavioural Science, Legal, and Underwriting to translate complex business requirements into customer-centred solutions. Research is a core part of my process, and I regularly lead qualitative and quantitative studies using tools like Askable, SurveyMonkey, Miro/FigJam, and Tealeaf. Insights from this work are shared portfolio-wide to inform broader product strategy, while analytics and heat-mapping help identify drop-offs and friction points, guiding iterative improvements to journeys such as Sum Insured through refined information architecture, visual hierarchy, and validation patterns.

I also contribute to NRMA’s design system and Help theme, ensuring experiences meet accessibility standards (WCAG 2.0 AA) and scale consistently across products. Alongside this, I actively integrate AI into my workflow to accelerate research synthesis, ideation, and early prototyping, allowing me to move faster from insight to execution. Beyond delivery, I regularly present to senior stakeholders, facilitate discovery and ideation sessions, and support onboarding and mentoring, helping build shared understanding and elevate design practice across the team.
